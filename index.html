BOSTON

Why Come to Boston?

Boston is both a modern and historical city. You can explore the city and see landmarks like the Prudential Center while learning about the history of the founding of the United States.

These are just some of the sites you can visit in this city:

Freedom Trail
This 2.5-mile-long trail will lead you to 16 locations rich in our country's history.
Prudential Center
One of the tallest buildings in the city, it has shops, restaurants, and even an observation deck.
Museum of Fine Arts
The 5th-largest museum in the country, it has over 450,000 works of art from a wide variety of movements and cultures.
